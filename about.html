About Palm Springs Properties

Your trusted partner in Eldoret's real estate market

Who We Are

Palm Springs Properties Limited Company is a premier real estate firm based in Eldoret, Kenya. Established with a vision to transform the real estate landscape in Uasin Gishu County, we have grown to become one of the most trusted names in property sales, rentals, and investment advisory services.

Our team consists of experienced real estate professionals who understand the local market dynamics and are committed to providing exceptional service to our clients. We specialize in residential and commercial properties across Eldoret, with particular expertise in prime areas such as Annex, Pioneer, West Indies, and the Central Business District.

Since our inception, we have successfully facilitated over 500 property transactions, helping families find their dream homes and investors identify lucrative opportunities. Our deep understanding of the Eldoret market, combined with our commitment to transparency and professionalism, sets us apart in the industry.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional real estate services that exceed client expectations while contributing to the sustainable development of Eldoret and its surrounding areas. We are committed to making property ownership accessible, transparent, and rewarding for all our clients.

Our Vision

To be the leading real estate company in Western Kenya, recognized for our integrity, innovation, and client-centered approach. We envision a future where every family can find their perfect home and every investor can achieve their financial goals through real estate.
