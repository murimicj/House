Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to buy a property?

The property buying process typically takes 4-8 weeks from offer acceptance to completion, depending on financing and documentation requirements.

Do you charge for property valuations?

We offer free property valuations for clients looking to sell through our agency. Independent valuations for other purposes may incur a fee.

What areas do you cover in Eldoret?

We cover all major areas in Eldoret including Annex, Pioneer, West Indies, CBD, and surrounding areas within Uasin Gishu County.

Do you help with property management?

Yes, we offer comprehensive property management services including tenant finding, rent collection, maintenance coordination, and regular property inspections.

What documents do I need to buy property?

You'll need a valid ID, proof of income, bank statements, and if applicable, mortgage pre-approval. Our team will guide you through all documentation requirements.

Do you offer investment advice?

Absolutely! Our experienced team provides investment advice, market analysis, and helps identify properties with good rental yields and capital appreciation potential.
